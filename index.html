Frequently asked questions

Quick answers about adoption, donations, and volunteering.

What are your adoption fees?

Fees vary by age and medical history; they cover vaccinations, microchipping, and spay/neuter.

Do you allow out-of-area adoptions?

Yes, with a virtual home check and transport coordination when needed.

Are donations tax-deductible?

In many regions yes; please consult your local regulations. Receipt provided upon request.

How can I volunteer?

Apply via the volunteer form; we offer roles in fostering, transport, events, and digital support.
